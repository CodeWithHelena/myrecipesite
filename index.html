Delicious Spaghetti Reciepe

Ingredient

  • 1lb usually 1box of thin spaghetti
  • 3/4 cups of chicken stock, i used a home made stock
  • 5 large about 2 lbs plum/roma tomatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers
  • 3 table spoon bovillion powder
  • 1 table spoon dried thyme
  • 1 table spoon curry powder
  • A small handfull of basil(optional)
  • salt to taste

Directions

  • Blend the tomatoes, onion, and peppers until smooth.
  • Boil down the tomatoes sauce on in a pot on medium-high heat until the sauce is about half the ammount, and has thickened significantly to look like a paste. This takes about 15-minutes, depending on how high the heat is.
  • Be sure to stir the sauce occasionally to prevent it from burning.
  • Once the sauce is reduced, pour in the stock, oil thyme curry powder and bovillon. At this stage, taste the sauce and djust for salt.
  • Add the spaghetti, pushing it down or breaking it to fit the pot.
  • Cover and allow to cook for 5 minutes on low-medium heat.
  • After 5 minutes the pasta should be soft enough to stir. Stir, turn down the heat to low, and allow to cook for another 8-10 minutes.
  • After 8 minutes, stir, and the pasta should be cooked. if it is not, add in a splash (about 1/4 cup) of water and continue to cook for another 5 minutes.
  • If you choose to add basil, add it in at this stage, then serve.
